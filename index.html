About JS Clock

A minimalist PWA digital clock optimized for repurposing old smartphones and tablets.

🎯 Core Feature: Intuitive Dimming

When used as a nightstand clock, even the faintest light can disrupt your sleep. With JS Clock, simply swipe up or down anywhere on the screen to instantly adjust the brightness. No need to fiddle with physical buttons or drop down the status bar.

✨ Key Features

  • Offline Support: Works perfectly without an internet connection once installed.
  • Wake Lock: Prevents the screen from turning off automatically while the clock is running.
  • Ad-Free & Lightweight: Designed with only essential features for your undisturbed sleep and focused time.
